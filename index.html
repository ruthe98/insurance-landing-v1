Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pet insurance.

How will the deductible work?

Your deductible is the amount you pay out of pocket before your insurance coverage kicks in. It's an annual deductible, meaning you only have to meet it once per year, not per incident. With a $750 deductible, once you've paid $750 in eligible vet expenses during your policy year, we'll start covering 90% of eligible costs for the rest of the year.

What is the 3-year price lock guarantee?

We guarantee that your monthly premium will not increase for 3 years from your enrollment date. This applies as long as you maintain continuous coverage. After the 3-year period, any rate adjustments will be communicated at least 6 months in advance.

How fast will claims be paid?

We pay the vet directly before you leave the clinic, so there's no traditional "claim" process for you to worry about. When you visit a vet, simply let them know you have our insurance. They'll verify your coverage through our system, provide treatment, and then bill us directly for the covered portion. You only pay your portion (deductible and coinsurance) at checkout. This eliminates the typical reimbursement waiting period that can take weeks with other pet insurance companies.

What is the 24/7 Triage Concierge?

It's an always-on chat-based service that helps you gauge how urgent your pet's issue is. Send symptoms (text, photo) any timeâ€”our AI triages them, and a licensed vet-tech verifies the advice within 15 min. For urgent cases we will show you the four nearest clinics with live wait-times, book your visit, pre-send records, and give you a cost + insurance-coverage estimate before you leave home.
